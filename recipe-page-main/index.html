Simple Omellete Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classcic omelette combiones beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats

    Preparation time
  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, cliced vegatables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  • Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed. You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffer texture.
  • Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  • Cock the omelette: Once the butter is melted and bubbling, pour in the eggs. Tilt the pon ti ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.
  • Add fillings (optional): When the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly. runny in the middle. sprinkle your chosen fillings over one half of the omelett.
  • Fold and serve: As the omelette continouse to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings. Let it cook for another minute, the slide it onto a plate.
  • Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
